Blockedt!

2017, 8 minutes, Color, Stereo, English

“Blockedt! is an 8-minute video and app project created in 2017/2018 in which Musson, taking on the persona of visionary CEO Guy White, pitches a functionless “anti-social social networking” app co-developed with Jonah Peretti. The app consists simply of a blank white screen meant for endless scrolling, with no visible content, no other users, and no conventional social interaction. Through this absurd proposal the work satirizes tech start-up culture, our endless desire for connection, and the design-driven emptiness of many digital interfaces. The blank white interface implicitly invokes whiteness as default while commenting on digital fatigue, endless feeds, and the ways social media captures our time and attention. By stripping away content entirely, Blockedt! forces viewers to reflect on what we actually do when we “scroll,” how we value connection, and how we might be complicit in our own distraction.”
